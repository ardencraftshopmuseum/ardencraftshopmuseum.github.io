Support

Our Web support services generally include technical support and online documentation.

Technical Support

If you ever find yourself in a jam, or need additional help, feel free to contact our IT Help Support Center.  We have a wide range of expertise in Information Technologies and we strive to make you more efficient and more productive in your job.

Three ways to request help:

  • Submit a Request for Help Form
  • Email us (consult@udel.edu)
  • Call us (831-6000)

Online Documentation

If you find that you simply need a refresher or would prefer reading documentation, we do have a number of online resources that can get you started.  Our first recommendation would be to visit the IT Help pages (www.udel.edu/help).  If you cannot find what you are looking for, we also have a number of external resources, including written documentation, videos, and slides that you may find useful.

We also offer a number of free classes and online training videos.
